Title: Options Introduction（期權概論）

English:
Options are derivative contracts granting the right, but not the obligation, to buy (call) or sell (put) an underlying asset at a specified strike price before or at expiration. Options serve as instruments for hedging, speculation, and income generation in modern markets.

中文：
期權是一種衍生性金融合約，賦予持有人在到期前或到期時以特定行使價格買入（看漲期權）或賣出（看跌期權）標的資產的權利（非義務）。期權廣泛用於避險、投機與收益策略，是現代金融市場的重要工具。
